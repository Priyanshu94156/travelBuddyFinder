Timoo is an online platform which aims in finding the travel companion. There are two different category of trip you can create Date Trip or Friend Trip.

If you are bored with normal dating then why don't you travel to some unexplored destination with the person who has the right chemistry with you. Date trips takes you to the new experience of the travel dating. If you are just a solo traveler and you want to meet some other companion or groups then you can join them, what a way of making new Friends in the journey that you guys haven't explored. It is rightly said, Friends who travel together stays together.
